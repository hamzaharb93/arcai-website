AI Foundation

AI-first. Not AI-added.

Every competitor retrofitted AI onto legacy software. ARCAI was built from the ground up with AI as the foundation — not a feature.

Clinical Reasoning

A multi-layer decision framework that evaluates patients the way an experienced anesthesiologist thinks — assessing risk, predicting complications, and generating strategies in real time.

Voice Documentation

Speak freely in German or English during surgery. The AI transcribes, structures into clinical fields, and routes vitals, notes, and medications to the right place — hands-free.

Predictive Alerts

Continuous vitals analysis that identifies trends and anticipates problems — alerting your team minutes before values become critical, not after.

Connected Ecosystem

One patient, one record across all three apps. Post-surgical transfer from OR to ward happens with a single tap. Zero re-entry, zero data loss.

Drug Intelligence

Auto-calculated doses by weight and species. CRI rates, fluid plans, interaction checking, and formulary-aware suggestions — integrated directly into your workflow.

Clinical Analytics

Procedure durations, staff performance, treatment compliance, ward efficiency — operational data that clinic management has never had access to before.
